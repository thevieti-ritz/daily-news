Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

1. Who We Are

Leaked Archives ("we", "us", or "our") is an entertainment video platform that brings users the latest viral and leaked video content from around the world. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Leaked Archives, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Profile Information: Your display name, username, and optional profile picture.
  • Usage Data: Videos you watch, like, comment on, or download.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to keep you signed in and remember your preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Show you relevant video content
  • Allow you to comment, like, and download videos
  • Improve our platform and fix technical issues
  • Send you important account-related notifications
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access

4. How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard encryption and security. Your password is never stored in plain text — it is encrypted before being saved.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following limited cases:

  • Service Providers: Google Firebase (authentication and database hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your session. We do not use advertising cookies or sell cookie data to advertisers. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings, but this will sign you out of your account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

Leaked Archives is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Leaked Archives after changes means you accept the updated policy.
